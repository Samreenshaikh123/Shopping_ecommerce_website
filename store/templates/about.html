About Us

Welcome to Paradrise Mattress!
At Paradrise Mattress, we take pride in providing you with the finest quality mattresses for a blissful night's sleep.
~Our story began in the 1990s when our visionary owner, Mohammed Jaffer Shaikh's Father BabaSab Shaikh, established our humble mattress shop.
~Back then, everything was done manually, from crafting each mattress with meticulous attention to detail to personally assisting customers in finding their perfect sleep solution.
~Over the years, we have grown and adapted with the changing times. While we continue to maintain our commitment to craftsmanship and quality, we have also embraced the convenience of technology.
~Today, you can find us online, offering a seamless shopping experience to customers near and far.
~Our dedication to excellence remains unwavering, and we strive to provide you with the best sleep experience possible.
~Each mattress we offer is thoughtfully designed and made with premium materials to ensure optimal comfort and support.
~Customer satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do. Our knowledgeable team is always ready to assist you in choosing the ideal mattress that suits your unique needs.
~Whether you prefer the traditional in-store experience or the ease of online shopping, we are here to guide you every step of the way.
~We now provide the convenience of online shopping alongside our commitment to craftsmanship and quality. Find your perfect sleep solution with us today!
Thank you for choosing Paradrise Mattress as your sleep partner. We look forward to helping you achieve the sleep of your dreams .
